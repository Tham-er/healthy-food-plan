Healthy Food Plan

Breakfast:

  • Vegetable Omelette (eggs, bell peppers, spinach)
  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • Plain Yogurt with Berries

Morning Snack:

  • A handful of Almonds

Lunch:

  • Grilled Chicken Salad (chicken breast, lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber)
  • Light Vinaigrette Dressing
  • Quinoa as a side

Afternoon Snack:

  • Carrot and Celery sticks
  • Hummus as a dip

Dinner:

  • Grilled Salmon with a hint of lemon
  • Steamed Asparagus
  • Baked Sweet Potatoes

Evening Snack (if needed):

  • Greek Yogurt serving
